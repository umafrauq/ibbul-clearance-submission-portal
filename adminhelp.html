Admin Clearance Guide

Welcome, Admin ⚙️

This guide explains your role in managing student clearance submissions:

⚙️
  1. Create your admin account by clicking Get Started below.
  2. Log in using your admin credentials.
  3. View and verify submitted documents from Final Year students.
  4. Approve ✅ or Reject ❌ clearance requests after checking validity.
  5. Provide remarks or corrections where necessary.
  6. Monitor clearance progress across all departments and faculties.
